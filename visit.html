visit a star club

Want to check out a club before you start volunteering? Star Clubs love visitors.

Lots of people thinking about volunteering have told us that they’d like to see a club in action before they start their own, so we’ve started a special network of clubs all over the UK that people can visit.

What is a Star Club?

Star Clubs are Code Clubs who are committed to sharing their expertise to help grow the Code Club community:

  • Friendly volunteers who are ready to share their experiences and help you get started
  • Supportive and committed club hosts who believe in the value of Computer Science education
  • Children who are awesome digital makers

Their doors are open for visits from potential volunteers! Look for one near you on the map below.
